About Oral Cancer Detection

This application uses machine learning to analyze images of oral tissue and detect potential signs of oral cancer.

How it works

The system analyzes uploaded images of oral tissue and provides a prediction along with a confidence score. The analysis is based on machine learning models trained to recognize patterns associated with oral cancer.

Important Notes

Disclaimer: This application is for educational and research purposes only. It should not be used as a replacement for professional medical diagnosis. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns.
